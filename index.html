Specials:

The Bulbasaur!

The Bulbasaur is our plant based pizza, that comes with vegan mozzerella and dough, spinach, artachoke, garlic, and green peppers.


The Charmander!

This is our hottest pizza! We use a special tomoato sauce, that has ghost peppers put into it! Wow! That's HOOOOTTT! Loaded on top of this special sauce is a mix of mozzerella and asiago cheese, jalpenos, carlonia reapers, hot sasuge, and red chilli flakes are sprinkled on top! Think you can handle the heat?


The Squirtle!

The squirtle is our sauciest pizza! This pizza comes with alfredo sauce as the base, has the same mix of mozzerella and asiago cheese that the Charmander comes with. With no other tradtional toppings adding, we put a swirl of pesto, buffalo, AND garlic parmisian on top, making this the most moist pizza you've ever seen! But we're not done there! This pizza also comes with pizza sauce on the side for you to dip your pizza slices in!


The Pokeball!

The Pokeball is a half cheese, half pepparoni pizza, that is split down the middle with a small circle made of olives.
